Ziyi Yuan is an interactive media artist currently pursuing an MPS at NYU ITP.

Her work investigates the tension between technology and the human body using real-time interaction, generative visuals, and physical installation to explore identity, surveillance, and emotional experience. She also practices graphic design with a focus on digital interfaces and visual systems.

Tools: TouchDesigner · Figma · Python · Arduino · Unity · Blender
